Ilyas (online known as Keyzz). A 13 year old kid born in Austin, Texas, is currently making his way up to the top of the eSports community as he started with nothing but a Nintendo Switch and a dream. After 2 years on Switch, his neighbor had given him a laptop from 2014. As he had received this in early 2022, the laptop was close to 9 years old. The game he originally played on this laptop was Minecraft: Java Edition. Then, once he found out he can use Nvidia GeForce NOW on his laptop, he started playing Fortnite Battle Royale. He had also bought a $0.99 keyboard from his local thrift store, with a office mouse his father has offered him. After a year of use of his laptop, his uncle had bought him enough PC parts for him to build one himself. After full building it on July 1st, 2023, He started playing non-stop and then after 4 months of playing, his YouTube channel was born on Oct 14th, 2023. Now after 2 years of being a content creator, Ilyas has been able to join Team Sarrow, 2AM Esports, and Beneath Reality, As well as befriending multiple known Fortnite players in the community. Ilyas also owns a website (keyzzilla.com) Which is currently under development for a new redo on the page.
