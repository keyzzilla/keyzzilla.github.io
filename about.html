👋 who am i?

i'm ilyas, better known as keyzzilla. i'm a 13 year old creative developer and designer based in austin, texas. i specialize in crafting high performance digital experiences that blend aesthetic beauty with technical precision.

my journey started with a nintendo switch and a passion for gaming, which evolved into a career in vfx editing and web development. i own teammonstergg and have worked as a creative for major esportsorgs like team sarrow and 2am esports.

💡 focus & vision

i'm currently focused on glassmorphism, terminal UI design, and web accessibility. i believe that the web should be both visually stunning and inclusive for everyone.

my current projects include velozzy (a custom chromium browser) and zillaai (a discord integration assistant). I also maintain the universal manual archive at manualkey.org.

outside of tech, i'm an athlete. i play soccer and flag football (currently holding a 4-0 record). i value the discipline and teamwork that sports provide.
